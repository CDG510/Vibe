About Vibe

Vibe is a music service dedicated to getting musicians connected and creating art like never before

By streamlining the booking process for recording studios, studios can focus less on finding work, and focus more on creating the best product ossible.

Artists don't have to worry about finding a place to record, as Vibe sources all available recording studios, searchable by area, genre, or price
