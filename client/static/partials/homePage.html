About Vibe

Vibe is a music service dedicated to getting musicians connected and creating art like never before.

By streamlining the booking process for recording studios, studios can focus less on finding work, and focus more on creating the best product possible.

Artists don't have to worry about finding a place to record, as Vibe sources all available recording studios, searchable by area, genre, or price.

Artists

Find studios to work with, see their schedule, and book time to record.

Who made this?

After being in a band and going through the hassle of finding a studio, working out times and everything else that goes into recording process, Vibe was created to make something that would make the already difficult lives of musicians easier, while helping studios who may not be as well-known as others.

After gaining the skills to put the site together, Vibe was born.
